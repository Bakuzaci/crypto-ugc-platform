## Why Crypto Projects Choose CryptoNoise

Traditional influencer marketing is broken for Web3. Upfront fees with no accountability. Fake followers. Zero conversion tracking. You deserve better.

**CryptoNoise flips the model:** Creators earn when you grow. Our performance-based marketplace means:

- **No upfront creator fees** - Pay only when content delivers views and conversions
- **Verified Web3 creators** - Every creator understands crypto and has proven engagement
- **Transparent attribution** - Know exactly which video drove each wallet connection
- **Automated everything** - From creator matching to content approval to payouts

## How It Works

### For Crypto Projects

**1. Create Your Campaign** (5 minutes)
<br>Set your budget, define your conversion goals (app installs, wallet connections, sign-ups), and upload your brand assets. Our campaign builder walks you through everything.

**2. Creators Apply & Create** (24-48 hours)
<br>Vetted creators who match your niche apply to your campaign. Approve them, and they start creating authentic short-form videos using your templates and talking points.

**3. Content Goes Live** (Immediate)
<br>Creators post across TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Twitter/X. Each post includes unique tracking links tied to their account.

**4. Pay for Performance** (Automatic)
<br>You're charged based on verified views and conversions. Our platform handles all creator payouts automatically - in USDC or fiat.

### For Creators

**1. Link Your Socials** - Connect your TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter accounts
<br>**2. Browse Campaigns** - Find crypto projects that match your audience and content style
<br>**3. Create & Post** - Follow campaign guidelines, make authentic content, post to your channels
<br>**4. Get Paid** - Earn per 1K views or per conversion. Withdraw anytime in USDC or to your bank.
